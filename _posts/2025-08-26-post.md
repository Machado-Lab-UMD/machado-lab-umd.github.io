We’re excited to share that **Dr. Carlos Machado** recently hosted a Reddit AMA (Ask Me Anything), where he answered questions from the public about evolutionary genetics, genomics, and the fascinating world of figs and fig wasps.  

It was a great opportunity to engage a broad audience, discuss ongoing research, and highlight the importance of studying biodiversity and species interactions.
